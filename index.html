03 — Two Ways to Close

Your close, your call

The way you close today — taking the card on the call, finishing the sale in one motion — doesn't have to change. Shopify supports it directly, and adds an optional second path for the moments when it fits.

Option A · Recommended

Close on the call (keyed entry)

  • ✓ Customer reads their card number while you're still on the phone
  • ✓ You key it directly into a Shopify Draft Order in the admin — payment processes instantly
  • ✓ Same one-motion close you do today: no link sent, no hangup window, no rethink risk
  • ✓ Sale fully recorded in Shopify: customer profile, order, inventory, reporting all build from this moment forward
  • ✓ No special app required — native Shopify Admin
  • ✓ Rate: 2.5% + $0.30 on Shopify Advanced (card-not-present rate)
Option B · Situational

Send a secure payment link

  • ✓ After the call, you email or text the customer a personalized payment link
  • ✓ Best for after-hours follow-ups, international buyers, or customers who prefer to pay on their own
  • ✓ Lets you capture intent without playing phone tag later
  • ✓ Customer-paid checkouts improve Google ad attribution and retargeting
  • ✓ Same Draft Order, same admin, same record-keeping as Option A
  • ✓ Rate: 2.5% + $0.30 on Shopify Advanced — identical to keyed entry
→

On the card-not-present rate: Any transaction where the card isn't physically present (keyed-in phone orders, online checkout, payment links) is treated as "card-not-present" by Visa/Mastercard. That's an interchange rule across every processor, not a Shopify rule. You're already paying CNP rates on phone closes today, whatever you use. On Shopify Advanced at 2.5% + $0.30, both close options come in at the same rate. There's no penalty for keeping your current motion.

04 — Return Rates

Industry returns vs. your model

Returns are the most cited risk when moving luxury goods online. Your pre-sale measurement process eliminates the root cause before it can happen.

Segment What drives returns Avg return rate
Standard apparel online Fit unknown, customers bracket sizes 25–40%
Luxury outerwear online Drape, warmth, weight can't be assessed from photos 28–34%
Luxury fashion broadly Deliberate buyers, high-stakes decisions 15–20%
Made-to-measure / custom fit Fit pre-confirmed before order placed <5%
Kaufman Furs — online with current process Measurement + photo confirmation before every sale ~0%
→

Key insight: Your 0% return rate isn't a product of selling in-store — it's a product of your process. That process travels online. Shopify doesn't change how you sell; it expands where and when you can sell.
